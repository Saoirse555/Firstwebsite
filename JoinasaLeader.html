Join as a Leader

Most people who join as Leaders are parents who want to move their child up the list.Although some join as Rovers to help the community and scouts that helped them.

What do I do as a Leader?

Each week you attend the meetings. In the meetings you help the children learn and engage and build new skills.Every two weeks or monthly depending on the group, you meet up and plan/organise the term so that every week something that covers the 6 SPICES.

How do I apply?

The best/easiest way to apply is to contact 4th Kildare Town scouts by email or phone number. However, be aware all Leaders and people who work for 4th Kildare Town Scouts are all voluntary non-paid workers.

Training to be a Leader

We are aware that we are constantly learning as people, in this way the inital training weekend, takes up a full weekend to train yourself up, it is an overnight. On this weekend, you learn all the basics of scouting, it is designed for those who never have scouted before, but also to allow those who have been scouts to learn more and develop new skills.
