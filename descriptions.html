Below are the promises that each section has!!

  • This is the Beavers Promise. To be said at the start of each meeting.
  • Beavers

    I promise to do my best,
    to be a good Beaver Scout,
    to love the world, and
    to love one another

  • This is the Cubs Promise. To be said at the start of each meeting.
  • Cubs

    On my honour I promise that I will do my best,
    to do my duty to God,
    to serve my community,
    to help other people and
    to live by the Cub Scout Law.

  • This is the Scouts Promise. To be said at the start of each meeting.
  • Scouts

    On my honour I promise that I will do my best,
    to do my duty to God,
    to serve my community,
    to help other people and
    to live by the Scout Law.

  • This is the Venture Promise. To be said at the start of each meeting.
  • Ventures

    On my honour I promise that I will do my best,
    to do my duty to God,
    to serve my community,
    to help other people and
    to live by the Venture Scout Law

  • This is the Rovers Promise. To be said at the start of each meeting.
  • Rovers

    On my honour I promise that I will do my best,
    to do my duty to God,
    to serve my community,
    to help other people and
    to live by the Rover Scout Law.

Below are the laws that each section has!!

  1. This is the Beavers Law. To be said at the start of each meeting.
  2. Beavers

    Are Friendly, they always say Hello, Please and Thank You!
    Are Kind, they work hard and help their family and friends.

  3. This is the Cubs Law. To be said at the start of each meeting.
  4. Cubs

    Are Friendly,
    Are Kind,
    Are Honest, they always tell the truth.
    Can be Trusted, they always keep their word .

  5. This is the Scouts Law. To be said at the start of each meeting.
  6. Scouts

    Are Friendly,
    Are Kind,
    Are Honest,
    Can be Trusted,
    Are Respectful, they care for themselves and for others.
    Are Brave, they stand up for right and good.

  7. This is the Venture Law. To be said at the start of each meeting.
  8. Ventures

    Are Friendly,
    Are Kind,
    Are Honest,
    Can be Trusted,
    Are Respectful,
    Are Brave,
    Are Loyal, they support their family, friends and others.

  9. This is the Rovers Law. To be said at the start of each meeting.
  10. Rovers

    Are Friendly,
    Are Kind,
    Are Honest,
    Can be Trusted,
    Are Respectful,
    Are Brave,
    Are Loyal,
    Seek out Justice for all, they strive to make the World a better place.
